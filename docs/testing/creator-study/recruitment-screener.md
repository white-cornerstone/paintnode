# Creative Blueprint recruitment screener rubric

Use the questions in the main protocol verbatim. Store raw answers and contact
details only in approved private storage. The repository-safe recruitment
matrix receives categories, never raw answers.

## Eligibility decision

Include only when every condition is true:

- creates or adapts visual assets at least monthly;
- used a desktop image, design, or content-production tool within three months;
- has not contributed to, implemented, previously tested, or already seen the
  Creative Blueprint facilitator script;
- agrees to use the supplied non-confidential Product rather than client work;
- can participate with the offered session setup and any privately arranged
  accommodation.

Exclude when any condition above is false. Do not weaken the rules to fill the
cohort. A declined recording is not an exclusion.

## Sampling buckets

- `multi_format_regular`: regularly adapts one concept to multiple formats.
- `ai_experience`: `never`, `occasional`, `monthly`, `weekly`, or `daily`.
- `keyboard_or_accessibility_coverage`: `true` only when the session actually
  exercises keyboard-heavy work or an arranged accessibility method. Never
  infer a diagnosis or accommodation from this aggregate flag.

Before scheduling the final cohort, confirm 6–8 eligible participants, at least
four multi-format regulars, at least two weekly AI users, and at least two
occasional or non-users. Seek keyboard/accessibility coverage; if recruitment
does not permit it, record a separate `keyboardOrAccessibilityCoverage`
exception decision privately and report only its de-identified `CB-DEC-N`
reference and rationale-recorded state. A cohort-mix shortfall requires its own
independent `cohortMix` exception. Neither decision can waive the other.
